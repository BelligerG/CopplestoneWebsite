All Stars After School Club

All Stars After School Club provides care for pupils at Copplestone Primary School at the end of the school day in a comfortable, fun and safe environment. The activities and opportunities at All Stars aims to nurture each child and complement and support their learning. The club enables children to take part in extra-curricular activities which focus on social, physical, academic, and spiritual development.

All Stars sessions run from 3:30‐5:30pm, Monday to Friday during term time.

For more information, bookings, forms, activities offered please visit our All Stars website.
