A message from our new Children and Youth worker

 

Hi everyone

My name’s Mark Wiliams, I’m married to Becky and we have a 3 year old daughter. For the past 16 years I’ve been working for a Christian charity helping churches throughout GB to run community football projects. We’ve been living in South Manchester and Becky has been working as a Teaching Assistant in a local primary school. We’re really excited to be moving down to Devon as I take on the role of your Children and Youth Worker. We’ve both worked with children and youth for many years and we are really looking forward to meeting you all and working alongside you.

We are busy looking for somewhere to live right now but we are excited to see what God has in store for us all in 2020 and beyond.

God bless

Mark
