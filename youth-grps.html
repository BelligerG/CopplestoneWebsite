The Grid

When: Sunday 10:45am‐12:00pm

The Grid is for you if you are between 11 and 16. With the rest of the Youth Church we join the adults in the main Church for the first few minutes of the service, and then we meet upstairs. The Grid is a great way to get involved in our exciting Sunday programme, and gives you a chance to meet people your own age, study the Bible and relate it to real life issues.

Energize

When: Fortnightly term time only Fridays 7:00‐8:30pm

Energize is a youth club for those from Year 6 to Year 9. We meet weekly, in the community room at Copplestone Church. We have; table tennis, pool, Wii, football table and group games. We also have craft and cookery session. Once per month we also meet with youth from the local area across the Ringsash Circuit. There are also opportunities to go on a summer camps and outings during the summer months.

Pulse

When: Fortnightly Tuesdays 7:00‐8:30pm

The Pulse is our youth group for Year 7 onwards. Every other Tuesday we have a lot of fun exploring more about our Christian faith. It is an evening of fun, friendship and more often than not... food! From themed nights to Quiz nights, from Challenges to competitions, from beach nights to Sports nights and from discussions to prayers ‒ this group is not to be missed.

Inter‐youth Nights

When: Every 3 months (various dates and times)

Join with other young people from the Ringsash Methodist Circuit for an evening of games, competitions and challenges. A great night to catch up with old and new friends.
