How to find us

The village of Copplestone is in Mid-Devon, four miles west of Crediton on the A377. Copplestone Methodist Church is opposite the Cross Hotel pub. You can park behind the church or in the village car park on the right up Bewsley Hill.

Booking of church premises

At the rear of the church is a large community room with a fully equipped kitchen, disabled access and toilets, which is available to hire for private functions. The cost of hire is £15.00 for the first two hours (minimum) and £5.00 per hour thereafter.

For bookings please contact Maureen Burlton 01363 881154.
